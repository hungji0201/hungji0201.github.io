ABOUT US

CANA is a regional blockchain technology team of a carbon-neutral planting company in southeast Asia with a legal license. CANA has received more than US$100 million in capital injections from several American foundation associations, and the company is dedicated to developing and strengthening the use of biotechnology in agriculture through digitization, asset-backed securitization, trading carbon emission reduction mechanism credits, and innovative financial technology. We combine Plantation, travel consumption, and DEFI (decentralized finance) into a single Web 3.0 ecosystem.We created the stable currency CUSD. You can pay with CUSD at any of our alliance merchant，implements the utilization of your digital asset CUSD in real-life daily consumption, such as travel accommodations, entertainment, online and offline commodity consumption, and many more.

Carbon and Virtual Currency Integration

Singapore has launched a voluntary carbon trading market established in collaboration with the stock exchange, DBS Bank, and Standard Chartered Bank. CANA token

CANA Token, a token within the CANA DeFi ecosystem, can be used in our online virtual store for purchases or participation in metaverse farming games. It can also be utilized on the decentralized exchange Pancakeswap. Members holding a certain amount of CANA Tokens qualify to receive dividends from the CANA DeFi ecosystem. Additionally, they possess decision-making and voting rights for future platform initiatives.
