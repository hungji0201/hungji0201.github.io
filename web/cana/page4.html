CANA Token & Roadmap

CANA Token

Tokens from the CANA DeFi ecosystem can be traded on the Pancakeswap decentralised exchange and used in the future in our metaverse online virtual store to buy NFT seeds and land, as well as participate in the metaverse planting game.
To ensure a continuous rise in currency prices and a gradual reduction in supply, a portion of the earnings from the sale of carbon credits will be used to repurchase and burn a substantial portion of the CANA Tokens total supply.
Members who possess a particular number of CANA Tokens are eligible for income dividends in the CANA DeFi ecosystem and are given voting and decision making rights to take part in the platform's future development.
