Welcome to CANAVERSE

CANA is a certified regional blockchain technology team that works with a south-east Asian company that plants trees to reduce carbon emissions. CANA is committed to advancing and enhancing the use of biotechnology in agriculture through digitization, asset-backed securitization, trading carbon emission reduction mechanism credits, and cutting-edge financial technology. The company has received more than $100 million in capital injections from various American foundation associations. We create a single Web 3.0 ecosystem that combines DEFI (decentralised finance), Plantation, and travel consumption. You now have a CANA Plant NFT that acts as proof of ownership of a physical plant and entitles the holder to a share of the profits from trading carbon emission reduction mechanism credits when we convert the physical assets of the plantation into a CANA Plant NFT. CANA Plant NFT is to store carbon emission data or certificates about your physical property, making it a physical asset. Staking CANA Plant NFT can result in monthly dividend returns of USDT and CUSD.
